The Long Trail

My trail journal for Vermont's Long Trail.

Section 1

September 30 – October 3, 2023; solo

Day 1: rain in the morning, very muddy. Lots of old logging roads. 4 miles of approach on Appalachian Trail from North Adams, MA. 10 miles on LT.

Day 2: dry and clear. Steep around the Route 9 gap. Fern fields on Harmon Hill, quartz boulders in Glastenbury wilderness. Glastenbury Mountain fire tower closed. 15 miles on LT.

Day 3: hot. Golden birch leaves. 13 miles on LT. Camped on summit of Stratton Mountain, watched sunset from fire tower.

Day 4: Sunrise from Stratton Mountain. Bridge out north of Lye Brook wilderness. Beautiful views on Old Rootville road. 13 miles to finish at Vermont Route 11/30.
