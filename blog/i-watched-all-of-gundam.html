I watched all of Gundam

Over the past 14 months I watched every TV episode, OVA, and movie in the Mobile Suit Gundam franchise in production order. I've seen plenty of mecha anime before, but this was the first time I had seen anything from the biggest real robot franchise in the world. And I have to say I was quite impressed. Gundam has its reputation for a reason, with incredible mechanical designs, compelling characters and classic stories that hold up to this day.

Some of it is certainly better than others. In general the main storyline (termed "Universal Century", or UC) and original creator (writer and director Yoshiyuki Tomino) produce the best works, but many of the spinoffs are worth watching too.

Here's my subjective ranking of each entry. It doesn't feel right to compare TV shows with movies and OVAs when their budgets, structure, and audiences are so different, so I've ranked each format separately. Likewise the Build series of gunpla-focused spinoffs are so different from the standard Gundam formula that I've ranked these separately as well.
