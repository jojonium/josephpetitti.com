2024 in review

As 2024 comes to a close, I look back on what I did this year. I spent a lot of time hiking, probably more than any previous year. I got seriously into winter hiking for the first time, and went on my first winter backpacking trip. I finished climbing all 48 New Hampshire peaks higher than 4,000 feet in June. I climbed my first 4,000-footer in Maine, and went back to the Adirondacks for the first time in 7 years. I also went on my first multi-day kayak trip.

In raw numbers, I hiked on 33 days, climbed 54 mountains, and spent 11 nights outdoors. My total distance hiked for the year came out to 267 miles with 84,000 feet of elevation gain.
